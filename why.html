There is no way for a browser to communicate directly with another browser. The only way to send information to a server, and the server sends the information to another browser.
This means accounts need unique identification, hence the need to use email. Passwords are there to ensure no one else can access your bookmarks even if they have your email.

This also allows you to use Daxmarks to access your bookmarks anywhere in the world.

Your email will never be used for marketing purposes.
