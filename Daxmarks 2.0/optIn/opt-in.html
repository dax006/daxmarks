Consent to use Daxmarks Bookmarks Sync

How this extension collects, uses, stores, and shares or discloses information.


No personally identifying information is collected, ever.
No information is ever shared/disclosed with any other site, person, company, or anything, ever.
Your bookmarks are collected and stored on an external server.
Email and password are stored on your local internal browser storage.
Passwords are never stored on the external server. A crytographically secure hash is used in its stead.
The email and password are not used for anything other than to link your bookmarks with your account.
